## Why Psychology Matters in Trading

Most traders focus on strategy — entry signals, stop placement, risk-reward ratios. But research consistently shows that the gap between a trader's theoretical edge and their actual results is almost entirely behavioral.

Understanding *why* you deviate from your plan is the first step to closing that gap.

## What MAE and MFE Tell You

- **MAE (Maximum Adverse Excursion)**: the worst drawdown a trade reached before closing
- **MFE (Maximum Favorable Excursion)**: the best profit a trade reached before closing

Comparing your actual exits to MAE/MFE reveals whether you're cutting winners too early or holding losers too long — without relying on memory.
